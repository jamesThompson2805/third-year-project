 The electrical penetration graph or EPG is a system used by biologists to study the interaction of insects with plants. EPG data capture voltage changes of the electrical circuit that connects insects and their food source. ~\newline


We make two data sets out of these data, Insect\+EPGRegular\+Train and Insect\+EPGSmall\+Train.
