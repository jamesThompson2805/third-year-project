 The data are surface electromyography (s\+EMG) signal, which captures electric activity of groups of muscle at rest or during activity. Data collection involves five healthy subjects conducting six movements of hand grasps. Collection device is Delsys\textquotesingle{} 2-\/channel EMG system.
